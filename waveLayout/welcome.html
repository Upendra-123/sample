About Srila Prabhupada


His Divine Grace

A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada

Founder-Acharya of International Society for Krishna Consciousness


He is the formost Vedic Scholar and teacher, represents an unbroken chain of fully self-realized spiritual masters beginning with Lord Krishna Himself. He took Diksha from His Divine Grace Sripada Bhaktisiddhanta Sarasvati.

Lord Sri Krishna, the son of mother Yasoda and Nanda Maharaja, is the Supreme Personality of Godhead.


The Supreme Personality of Godhead said: Those who fix their minds on My personal form and are always engaged in worshiping Me with great and transcendental faith ae condidered by Me to be most perfect. [Bg.12.2]


Krishna is everywhere. Simply you have to catch Him. And He's also ready for being caught. Yes, if somebody wants to catch Him. Suppose you are a devotee. If you want to catch Him. He comes forward ten times than your desire. He's so kind. Therefore, we have to simply receive Him.


London, August 21, 1973
